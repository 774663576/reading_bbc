内容简介

为了提高服务质量，完善用户体验，许多科技公司需要人工手动完成成千上万个电脑无法完成的小任务。人们如何称呼做这些工作的人？本期 “地道英语” 介绍新词 “microworker” 的意思和用法。

Neil
A microworker? Is that someone who makes microwave ovens?

Feifei

Neil
And yes – the word 'micro' used as a prefix, indicates that something is small.

Feifei
但我们说的不是 “small workers” 小员工，我们说的是计算机无法独立完成的小型数字任务。Neil, stop typing!

Neil
Sorry… These might be things like drawing around a digital image of a face to help train artificial intelligence systems to recognise people.

Feifei
是的，“microworkers” 的工作可能包括通过执行简单、重复的数字任务，从而帮助改进计算机的算法。

Neil
Let's hear some examples.

Examples
Since the recession, lots of people who lost their jobs have earned cash as microworkers.

Some complain it's badly paid, but others like the flexibility of microwork.

Neil
